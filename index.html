Tennessee

A state offering a diverse range of attractions, from the music scene in Nashville, to the Great Smoky Mountains. Visit large citys to open farmland, and everything inbetween. You can visit Graceland, Dollywood, Grand Ole Opry, and the secret city of Oak Ridge, as it took part in the Manhattan Project.


If you look at the tourist sites, you get a sense that Tennessee, is all about music, history, celebrites: "Elvis, Dolly Parton, President Jackson and Megan Fox" come to mind. In moving here and living here I do not see that. I see living in the bible belt, 100's of different churchs, having dry counties, unique liquor laws, and in the rural areas a more family oriented, respectful way of life. There is those who want to change everything to what they feel is the "future" but most people just want to live on thier farm, watch their children and crops grow.
